---
title: "Bluplai modules: Features and access control"
description: "Explore the six modules that make up Bluplai — Home, Discover, Qualify, Plan, Engage, and Accounts — and learn how to control access for your team."
---

Bluplai is organized into modules, each covering a distinct area of the customer success workflow. Every module appears as a section in the sidebar. Admins control which modules each team member can access, so you can focus people on the parts of the product that are relevant to their role.

To manage module access, navigate to **Admin → Permissions**. See [Roles and permissions](/admin/roles-permissions) for step-by-step instructions.

<Tip>
  A good starting point is to enable all modules for your team and then restrict access as you identify which modules each person actually needs. It is easier to remove access later than to troubleshoot why someone cannot find a feature.
</Tip>

## Available modules

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Home">
    The Home module is your team's starting point. It brings together dashboards, analytics, and activity tracking in one place.

    **Sections:**
    - **Overview** — A high-level summary of activity and key metrics across your accounts.
    - **Analytics** — Aggregated data and charts to track performance trends over time.
    - **Activity Feed** — A chronological log of recent actions across accounts and projects.
    - **My Tasks** — A personal task list surfacing action items assigned to the signed-in user.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Discover">
    The Discover module is where you build a structured picture of each customer — their goals, challenges, and current state — before you start delivering value.

    **Sections:**
    - **Snapshot** — Key facts and context about the customer account.
    - **Goals** — The customer's strategic objectives and desired outcomes.
    - **Challenges** — Pain points and blockers mapped as opportunities.
    - **Planning Board** — A free-form space for notes and early-stage thinking.
    - **Workshop** — Structured outputs from discovery workshops and alignment sessions.
    - **Outcomes** — Documented outcomes tied to the customer's goals.
    - **Solutions** — Proposed solutions mapped to the customer's challenges.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Qualify">
    The Qualify module helps you build the business case for your solution and track the value you are delivering.

    **Sections:**
    - **Value Drivers** — The core drivers of value for the customer.
    - **Market Trends** — Relevant external trends that affect the customer's context.
    - **Outcomes** — Qualified outcomes with measurable targets.
    - **Technical Vision** — The customer's technical landscape and desired future state.
    - **Value** — Quantified value and ROI calculations.
    - **Investments** — Customer investment data for ROI analysis.
    - **Solution Fit** — How well your solution maps to the customer's needs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Plan">
    The Plan module covers everything you need to run a structured Customer Success Plan, from tasks and milestones to timelines and deliverables.

    **Sections:**
    - **Tasks** — Action items and to-dos linked to the customer plan.
    - **Success Plan** — The full Customer Success Plan (CSP) for the account.
    - **Timeline** — A visual timeline of key milestones and events.
    - **Actions** — High-level actions and next steps.
    - **Initiatives** — Strategic initiatives tied to the customer's goals.
    - **Milestones** — Key milestones and delivery targets.
    - **Deliverables** — Specific outputs and artifacts to be delivered.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Engage">
    The Engage module covers how you interact with customers — from health checks and surveys to workflows and adoption tracking.

    **Sections:**
    - **Digital Assessment** — Structured digital assessments for customer health and maturity.
    - **Pulse** — Health surveys, interviews, and satisfaction tracking.
    - **Workflows** — Automated and manual workflows for customer engagement.
    - **Events** — Customer-facing events and touchpoints.
    - **Adoption** — Product adoption tracking and reporting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Accounts">
    The Accounts module is your central view of all customer accounts and the relationships within them.

    **Sections:**
    - **All Accounts** — A list of all customer accounts in your organization.
    - **Projects** — Projects linked to customer accounts.
    - **Stakeholders** — Key contacts and decision-makers at each account.
    - **Roadmap** — A roadmap view of upcoming work and commitments.
    - **Partnership** — Partnership status and health for each account.
    - **Org Insights** — Organizational structure and insights for customer accounts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing module access

Navigate to **Admin → Permissions** to control which modules each team member can access. Use the toggle matrix to enable or disable modules per user. You can also expand each module to control access at the section level.

<Note>
  Users with the Admin role always have access to all modules. Module permission toggles only affect Editor and Viewer role members.
</Note>
