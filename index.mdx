Bluplai is a customer success platform built for teams who need to manage complex customer relationships at scale. From initial discovery through long-term adoption, Bluplai gives you the tools to track health, run structured success plans, measure ROI, and keep every stakeholder aligned — all in one place.

## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Go to your Bluplai URL and sign in with your email or Google account via the login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your organization">
    On first login you will be prompted to name your organization. All of your accounts, projects, and team members are scoped to this organization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first account">
    Navigate to **Accounts** and click **Add Customer** to create your first customer account. Then create a project to start tracking the engagement.
  </Step>
</Steps>
