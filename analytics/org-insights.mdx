---
title: "Org Insights: Portfolio-wide analytics and health"
description: "Explore aggregate performance, account health, stakeholder activity, and growth trends across your entire customer portfolio in one analytics view."
---

Org Insights gives you a deeper analytical view of your organization's entire customer portfolio. Where the Dashboard surfaces real-time counts and recent activity, Org Insights focuses on patterns and health signals across all accounts over time. Use it when you need to prepare for a QBR, build an executive report, or do a portfolio-wide health review.

To open Org Insights, navigate to **Accounts → Org Insights** in the sidebar.

## KPI metrics

The top of the page shows a grid of key performance indicators, each with a trend indicator (up, down, or neutral) so you can see whether things are moving in the right direction. These metrics cover the dimensions your leadership team cares most about: account volume, health scores, engagement, and growth signals.

## Charts and analytics

Org Insights includes six charts that cover different angles of your portfolio health.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Account Type Distribution">
    A donut chart showing how your accounts break down by type. Use this to understand the composition of your portfolio at a glance—for example, how many accounts are enterprise versus mid-market.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Growth Trend (6 months)">
    An area chart tracking account growth over the last six months. A rising line indicates you're adding accounts faster than you're churning them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Account Health by Account">
    A horizontal bar chart showing a health score for each account. Bars are color-coded — green for healthy, yellow for at-risk, red for critical. Use this to quickly spot accounts that need attention.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Stakeholder Activity">
    A grouped bar chart showing engagement activity per account. Declining activity can be an early churn signal, while spikes may indicate a renewal or expansion conversation is underway.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Engagement Rate">
    An area chart tracking overall engagement rates across your portfolio over time. A healthy portfolio maintains consistently high engagement. Dips here often point to accounts worth investigating.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Portfolio Health Score">
    A gauge showing an overall health score for your organization, aggregated from account health scores and engagement metrics. This is a useful headline number for executive reporting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Key insights and recommendations

Below the charts, Org Insights surfaces a set of insight cards that highlight notable patterns—such as accounts that recently became at risk, stakeholder engagement changes, or milestones reached. These cards are designed to prompt action, not just inform.

## Account performance summary

At the bottom of the page, a detailed performance table lists each account with key metrics side by side. Use this table for thorough portfolio reviews, or to identify accounts to call out in a QBR deck.

## Navigation within Org Insights

Use the section filter bar near the top of the page to jump between sections: **KPI Metrics**, **Charts & Analytics**, **Key Insights**, **Performance**, and **Recent Activity**. Clicking a section label scrolls the page to that section.

<Note>
  Org Insights shows aggregate metrics across all accounts. For a deep-dive into a single account—health score history, individual stakeholder activity, project details—navigate to that account's detail page instead.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Org Insights is a strong starting point for QBR prep. Combine the Growth Trend and Account Performance table to build a narrative around portfolio health before your next executive review.
</Tip>
