The Integrations tab in Administration is where you connect Bluplai to external tools and services your team already uses. Integrations allow data to flow between Bluplai and the rest of your toolchain — reducing manual work and keeping your customer context up to date.

To access the Integrations tab, navigate to **Admin** in the sidebar and click the **Integrations** tab.

<Note>
  The Integrations feature is currently in development. Full integration management will be available in an upcoming release. Contact your account representative to learn about integration options available on your plan or to request early access.
</Note>

## What integrations offer

When integrations are enabled for your organization, you can:

- Connect external tools and services to your Bluplai workspace.
- Sync customer data, contacts, and activity from connected systems.
- Trigger workflows in Bluplai based on events in external tools.
- Push Bluplai data (such as health scores or CSP updates) back to connected platforms.

<Tip>
  If you have a specific integration in mind that is not yet available, reach out to your account representative. Integration roadmap priorities are influenced by customer demand.
</Tip>

## Integration availability

Integration availability varies by plan. Not all integrations are available on every tier. If you need a specific integration that is not showing as available in your Admin panel, contact your Bluplai account representative to discuss options.

<Warning>
  Only users with the **Admin** role can manage integrations. Member-level users cannot view or configure the Integrations tab.
</Warning>
