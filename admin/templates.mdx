---
title: "Templates: Standardize and reuse team content"
description: "Create and manage reusable templates for discovery questionnaires, success plans, and other structured content to standardize your team's workflows."
---

Templates are reusable starting points for the structured content your team creates most often — discovery questionnaires, Customer Success Plans, surveys, execution playbooks, and reports. Instead of building these from scratch for every new account or project, you create a template once and reuse it across as many customers and projects as you need.

Templates are organized into three categories: **Discovery**, **Execution**, and **Reports**, reflecting the main phases of a customer success engagement.

To manage templates, navigate to **Admin** in the sidebar and click the **Templates** tab.

## Why use templates

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Consistency across your team" icon="users">
    Templates ensure every CSM follows the same structure for key deliverables, reducing variability in quality and coverage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Faster onboarding" icon="bolt">
    New team members can get productive immediately by selecting a template instead of figuring out what to include from scratch.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customer archetypes" icon="building">
    Build different templates for different customer segments — enterprise, mid-market, SMB — so each engagement starts from the right baseline.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Standardized reporting" icon="chart-bar">
    Report templates make it easy to produce consistent business reviews and health check outputs across all of your accounts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Template categories

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Discovery templates">
    Discovery templates pre-populate the structure for customer discovery sessions — questions to ask, sections to fill in, and frameworks to follow. Use them as a starting point when opening a new account or kicking off a new project.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Execution templates">
    Execution templates define the tasks, milestones, and deliverables for common engagement patterns. Apply them when setting up a Customer Success Plan or a new project to get a structured plan in place quickly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Report templates">
    Report templates provide a consistent structure for business reviews, health summaries, and other recurring outputs. Select a report template when generating a new report to start from a proven structure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Creating a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Admin → Templates">
    Navigate to **Admin** in the sidebar and click the **Templates** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Template">
    Click the **Create Template** button in the top-right corner of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the template details">
    Give your template a name and description, select a category (**Discovery**, **Execution**, or **Reports**), and fill in the template content — steps, sections, questions, or whatever structure you want to reuse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the template">
    Save the template. It is now available for your entire organization to use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using a template

When creating a new survey, discovery session, CSP, or report, look for the option to start from a template. Selecting a template pre-populates the content so you can review, adjust, and save — rather than building from a blank page.

<Tip>
  Build templates for your most common customer archetypes. For example, an "Enterprise Onboarding Template" might include a full set of discovery questions and a structured success plan skeleton, while an "SMB Discovery Template" might be leaner and more focused on quick time-to-value.
</Tip>

## Searching and filtering templates

The Templates tab includes a search bar and category filter buttons (**All**, **Discovery**, **Execution**, **Reports**) so you can quickly find the template you need. Type any part of a template's name into the search bar to filter the list.

## Previewing a template

Before using a template, you can preview its full content by clicking the preview icon on the template card. This lets you verify the structure and decide whether it is the right fit before applying it.
