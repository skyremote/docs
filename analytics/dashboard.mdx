The Dashboard is the first thing you see when you open Bluplai. It gives you a real-time summary of your entire customer portfolio: how many customers, projects, initiatives, and stakeholders you're managing, how engagement is distributed across lifecycle stages, and what your team has been up to lately. You can filter by date range, zero in on a single metric, or compare two time periods side by side—without ever leaving the page.

## Stat cards

At the top of the Dashboard, four stat cards give you instant counts across your portfolio. Click any card to navigate directly to the corresponding list.

| Card | What it counts |
|---|---|
| **Customers** | Total number of accounts in your organization |
| **Projects** | Total projects across all customers |
| **Initiatives** | Total initiatives tracked across all projects |
| **Stakeholders** | Total stakeholders mapped across all accounts |

<Tip>
  Click any stat card to navigate directly to the corresponding list — Customers, Projects, Initiatives, or Stakeholders.
</Tip>

## Charts

Three charts give you deeper context on how your portfolio is performing and where engagement is concentrated.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Activity Trend">
    An area chart showing the daily or weekly volume of activity across your organization. Use the time range selector to view the last **7, 30, 90, 180, or 365 days**. This chart is useful for spotting periods of high engagement or identifying slowdowns before they become problems.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Module Completion">
    A bar chart showing completion progress across Bluplai's key platform modules: Discovery, Workshop, CSP, ROI, Assessments, and Surveys. Click any bar to jump directly to that module.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Engagement by Lifecycle Stage">
    A pie chart showing how your projects are distributed across lifecycle stages (Discover, Evaluate, Consider, Buy, Onboard, Use, Adopt, Extend). Click any segment to filter the Projects list to that stage.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recent activity and projects

Below the charts, the Dashboard surfaces two feeds to help you stay on top of what's happening.

- **Recent Activity** — a live feed of the latest changes made across your organization. Click "View all" to open the full [Activity log](/analytics/activity).
- **Recent Projects** — a snapshot of your most recently created or updated projects, showing status, lifecycle stage, and initiative progress.

## Filtering

The Dashboard includes three ways to narrow down what you're looking at.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Date range filter">
    Select a start and end date to focus all stats and charts on a specific period. This is helpful when you want to review a quarter or a sprint, rather than your all-time totals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Stat filter">
    Focus the stat cards on a single metric—Customers, Projects, Initiatives, or Stakeholders—to reduce visual noise when you only care about one dimension.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Comparison mode">
    Enable comparison mode to set a second date range alongside your primary range. Each stat card then shows both values so you can see how performance changed between the two periods.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

To reset all filters at once, click **Clear filters**.

## Quick actions

At the bottom of the Dashboard, three quick-action buttons let you jump straight into common workflows without navigating through the sidebar.

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| Add Customer | Opens the new customer form |
| Create Project | Opens the new project form |
| Plan Initiatives | Takes you to the CSP initiatives tab |

<Note>
  The Dashboard is scoped to your organization. If you're working across multiple organizations, switch organizations using the selector in the sidebar before reviewing stats.
</Note>
