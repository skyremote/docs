Pulse brings together all of your customer feedback instruments in one place. Instead of managing interviews, surveys, and satisfaction checks across separate tools, you work from a single section with three focused tabs — each designed for a different kind of customer interaction.

## The three tabs

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Interviews" icon="clipboard-list">
    Structured questionnaire-based sessions with a customer contact. Track progress question by question.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Surveys" icon="message">
    Short-form surveys you share with customers via a public link. No login required for respondents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Satisfaction" icon="star">
    Rating-based questionnaires to measure CSAT, NPS, or other satisfaction dimensions over time.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Interviews

The **Interviews** tab shows all questionnaire-based interviews across your accounts. Each interview is associated with a customer and tracks response progress toward completion.

Two interview formats are available:

- **Standard questionnaire** — a fixed set of 15 structured questions covering common customer success topics
- **Custom interview** — a fully custom question set you define yourself

### Create an interview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pulse and go to the Interviews tab">
    Navigate to **Pulse** in the sidebar. The **Interviews** tab is selected by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new interview">
    Click **New Interview** and choose whether to use a standard questionnaire or a custom format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the customer">
    Associate the interview with the relevant customer account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in responses">
    Open the interview record and enter responses as you work through the session with the customer. The platform tracks progress as you answer each question.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as complete">
    Once all questions are answered, mark the interview as **Completed**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Interview status is automatically calculated from response progress:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Draft | No questions answered yet |
| In Progress | Partially answered |
| Completed | All questions answered (or manually marked complete) |

## Surveys

The **Surveys** tab is for short-form surveys that you share with customers via a public link. Surveys support multiple question types and can be associated with a project.

<Note>
  Customers do not need a Bluplai account to fill out a survey. Anyone with the public link can respond directly in their browser.
</Note>

### Create a survey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Surveys tab">
    In **Pulse**, click the **Surveys** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new survey">
    Click **New Survey**. Give the survey a title and optional description, then select a type (solution, feedback, assessment, or satisfaction) and optionally associate it with a project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add questions">
    Open the survey and add questions. Supported question types include text, multiple choice, and rating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the public link">
    Each survey has a unique public URL. Copy the link from the survey detail page and share it with your customer by email or chat. Customers can respond directly in their browser — no Bluplai account needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review responses">
    Responses appear in the survey detail view as customers submit them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Survey statuses

Surveys move through three statuses: **Draft** → **Active** → **Closed**. Only active surveys accept new responses via the public link.

<Tip>
  Associate the survey with a project so you can cross-reference responses alongside other project activity.
</Tip>

## Satisfaction

The **Satisfaction** tab is for rating-based questionnaires that measure how satisfied customers are across specific dimensions — such as NPS, deployment experience, support quality, or product experience.

### Available categories

Available satisfaction categories include:

- Net Promoter Score
- Deployment
- Engagement
- Adoption
- ROI
- Onboarding Experience
- Product Experience
- Support Experience

### Create a satisfaction questionnaire

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Satisfaction tab">
    In **Pulse**, click the **Satisfaction** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new questionnaire">
    Click **New Questionnaire**. Enter a title, optional description, select a category, and optionally associate it with a customer account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the public link">
    Like surveys, satisfaction questionnaires have a public link you can share with customers. Respondents submit ratings without needing to log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track scores over time">
    The satisfaction dashboard shows your average score across all questionnaires, so you can track sentiment trends across your portfolio.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The public survey link works for both survey and satisfaction types. Customers access it directly in their browser — no Bluplai account required.
</Note>
