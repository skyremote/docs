Bluplai is a customer success platform built for CS teams who manage complex customer relationships at scale. It brings your accounts, projects, success plans, stakeholder maps, health signals, and ROI data into one place — so your team can spend less time hunting for context and more time driving outcomes.

## How Bluplai is organized

Bluplai uses a four-level hierarchy to keep all your data structured and easy to navigate:

- **Organization** — your company's workspace. All data is scoped to your organization and only visible to your team members.
- **Accounts** — your customers. Each account has its own health score, lifecycle stage, stakeholder map, and project history.
- **Projects** — a unit of work inside an account. A project tracks a specific engagement, onboarding, or implementation with its own milestones and actions.
- **Initiatives** — high-level outcomes or goals that span a project. Initiatives break down into actions and deliverables that move the customer toward success.

This structure means you can track a single action all the way up to the customer relationship it belongs to, and understand its impact at every level.
