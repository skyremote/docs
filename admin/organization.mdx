An organization is the top-level container for everything in Bluplai — your accounts, projects, team members, permissions, and all associated data. When you sign in, you always operate within the context of one organization at a time. Every record you create, every customer you add, and every project you configure belongs to that organization and is visible only to members of it.

<Note>
  Data does not cross organization boundaries. If you belong to more than one organization, each one is fully independent — members, accounts, and settings are not shared between them.
</Note>

## How organizations work

When you first sign in to Bluplai, an organization is either created for you automatically or you are prompted to create one. From that point on, everything you do in the product is scoped to your active organization.

Key things to know:

- All team members within an organization share the same accounts, projects, and data.
- Roles and permissions are set per organization — a user who is an Admin in one org does not automatically have Admin access in another.
- You can belong to multiple organizations, but you can only work in one at a time.

## Creating an organization

The first time you sign in to Bluplai, you will be guided through creating your organization.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Bluplai">
    Complete the sign-in flow using your email or an authorized single sign-on provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your organization">
    Enter a name for your organization. This name appears throughout the product and in any invitation emails sent to your team. Choose something your teammates will recognize — typically your company name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using Bluplai">
    Once your organization is created, you land on the Home dashboard. You can invite team members straight away from **Admin → Invite Member**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching between organizations

If you belong to more than one organization, you can switch between them without signing out.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the organization switcher">
    Click your organization name or avatar in the sidebar. A dropdown lists every organization you belong to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an organization">
    Click the organization you want to switch to. Bluplai reloads and scopes all data to the selected organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Bluplai remembers which organization you last used, so you are automatically returned to it the next time you sign in.
</Tip>

## Organization name and settings

Admins can update the organization name at any time from the organization settings. Changes take effect immediately and are reflected across the product for all members.

<Warning>
  Only users with the **Admin** role can change organization settings. If you do not see the option, ask your organization admin.
</Warning>
