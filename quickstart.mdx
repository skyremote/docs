---

Getting up and running with Bluplai takes just a few minutes. This guide walks you through signing in, setting up your organization, adding your first customer account, creating a project, and inviting a teammate.

<Tip>
  If you'd like to explore the platform before adding real customers, use Demo Mode. It gives you a pre-loaded workspace with sample accounts, projects, stakeholders, and analytics — no setup needed. See [Explore Bluplai with Demo Mode](/demo-mode).
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Go to your Bluplai URL and sign in using your email address or your Google account. If you don't have an account yet, select **Sign up** on the login page to create one.

    After signing in, Bluplai loads your workspace. If this is your first time, you'll be taken to the dashboard to complete setup.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create or join an organization">
    All data in Bluplai is scoped to an organization — your team's shared workspace. On your first login you'll be prompted to name your organization.

    Enter a name for your organization and confirm. If you've been invited to an existing organization by a teammate, accept the invitation link from your email instead and you'll join their workspace automatically.

    <Note>
      You can belong to multiple organizations and switch between them from the sidebar at any time.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first account">
    Navigate to **Accounts** in the sidebar and click **Add Customer**. Fill in the account details:

    - **Name** — the customer's company name (required)
    - **Industry** — optional, but useful for filtering your portfolio
    - **Website** — optional link to the company's website
    - **Location** — optional city or region
    - **Description** — a brief note about the customer or the relationship

    Click **Create Customer** to save the account. You'll be taken to the account detail page where you can set the lifecycle stage, health score, and add stakeholders.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a project">
    From the account detail page, click **New Project**. Give the project a name that describes the engagement — for example, "Q1 Onboarding" or "Platform Expansion".

    Inside the project you can add:

    - **Milestones** — key checkpoints with due dates
    - **Actions** — specific tasks with owners
    - **Initiatives** — higher-level goals the project contributes to

    Projects keep all the work for a specific engagement in one place, tied directly to the customer account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite a team member">
    Navigate to **Admin** → **Team Members** and click **Invite Member**. Enter their email address and select a role, then send the invitation.

    They'll receive an email with a link to join your organization. Once they accept, they can sign in and access your shared workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Next steps

After completing setup, your Dashboard gives you an overview of account health, recent activity, and engagement across your portfolio. From there you can start building [Success Plans](/features/success-plans), running [Pulse surveys](/features/pulse), and tracking [ROI](/features/qualify) for your accounts.
